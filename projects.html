SOCO, CBC and Applications in Cloud Resources Provisioning

There is a long history of research on both Smoothed Online Convex Optimization (SOCO) and Convex Bodies Chasing (CBC). We are trying to figure out how the inherent connections between SOCO and CBC inspire new design of efficient and elegant algorithms with theoretical guarantees, under views from both online algorithms and online optimization. We also study widespread applications in cloud resources provisioning, where SOCO was originally proposed.
