Generally

I am always attracted by massive and uncertain objects, which I am obsessed with designing algorithms to deal with. To be specific, what I am most interested in is Algorithms Under Uncertainty and Algorithms for Massive Data. The former mainly deals with online decision making problems in the face of an ever-changing or even adversarial environment, including online optimization and online learning; the latter is mainly termed sublinear algorithms, including streaming algorithms (sublinear space), property testing (sublinear time) and compressed sensing (sublinear measurement), with techniques for lower bounds for these problems, specifically, communication complexity-derived lower bounds.
